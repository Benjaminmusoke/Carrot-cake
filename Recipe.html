Carrot cake History Recipe

Preparation time: 50 mins

Portions:

Cake


Ingredients

  • 250 g unsalted butter, softened
  • 250 g light brown soft sugar
  • 5 large free-range eggs
  • 1 orange, zest and juice off
  • 1 slightly heaped teaspoon baking powder
  • 100 g shelled walnuts, chopped, plus a handful for serving
  • 1 heaped teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground cloves)
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 250 g carrots, peeled and coarsely grated sea salt

Rate this recipe:

Method and directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius / 350 degrees Fahrenheit gas.

  2. Grease and line a 22cm-square cake tin or a round equivalent with a greaseproof paper.

  3. Beat the butter and sugar together by hand or in a food processor until pale and fluffy.

  4. Beat in the egg yolks one by one, and add the orange zest and juice.

  5. Stir in the sifted flour and baking powder, and add
    the ground almonds, walnuts, spices and grated carrot and mix together well.

  6. In a seperate bowl, whisk the egg whites with a pinch of salt
    unitl stiff, then gently fold them into the mix.

  7. Scoop the mixture into the prepared cake tin and cook
    in the preheated oven for about 50 minutes until golden and risen.

  8. You can check to see if the cake is cooked by poking a cocktail stick into it.

  9. Remove it after 5 seconds and if it comes out clean the cake is cooked;
    if slightly sticky, it needs a bit longer, so put it back in the oven.

  10. Leave the cake to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then turn it out
    on to a rack and rest for at least an hour
